With beautiful landscape, comes beautiful lodging to stay at. Canada has many nice and beautiful lodges to book, and for relatively affordable prices as well. Tripadvisor provides many of these lodges to stay at, as well as the prices and regulations. There are over 2,000 options for lodging, and there are also many options for transportation to and within the country.

Public transportation within Canada is primarily dominated by air travel. Air travel is the most popular and efficient way to get around the country, due to the large amounts of space between the the many cities in the country. According to the transportation page of the offical Candian Government website, air travel within the country requires a valid piece of photo identification as a resident, or a valid passport as a foreigner. Public transportation in Canada also includes busses, taxis, ferries, etc.

The immigration page of the offical Canadian Government website lists the requirements to enter the country. Some countries of origin require a valid travel documents, while some exceptions are made. Primarily, these exceptions are made for land travel from the US or sea travel. In order to enter the country, though, some valid form of identification and a passport are required. The country can be accessed via land travel from the US, air travel, or sea travel.
